--- Fuel purchase rebate program (KATS) ---

KATS (Keep All The Savings) is designed to encourage members to top-off
at outlying airports where fuel charges are less than our home base
airport. If the charge per gallon is less than OSU's, a KATS rebate
applies. If the charge per gallon is more than OSU's, a member may only
deduct the equivalent OSU fuel charge.

Purchase 80 octane at a cost below OSU's cost per gallon, for...

N12BC.......................................Keep all the savings!

Purchase 100 octane at cost below OSU's cost per gallon, for...

N1697X......................................Keep all the savings!
N64333......................................Keep all the savings!
N80624......................................Keep all the savings!
