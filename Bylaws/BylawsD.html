Upon acceptance of this money it is agreed that the above stated amount shall be credited to the member's "Prepaid Dry-Time" account. Thereafter, the member will be permitted to "fly off," at the member's discretion, their entire balance in that account. The only charges that will debit this member's "Prepaid Dry-Time" account shall be the following...

1) Monthly COFA dues., 2) Monthly COFA assessments.
3) Monthly "Dry-Time" charges for flights taken in COFA INC aircraft.

Until such time as the member's entire "Prepaid Dry-Time" fund is exhausted, the member shall be billed for fuel ÒwetÓ charges, and other expenses. The member is not required to fly any minimum amount each month. By virtue of this agreement the member has no privileges above and beyond any other member's privileges except as stated herein.

Should the member resign from COFA INC., encounter some unforeseen calamity, or should COFA INC. cease operation before the amount prepaid in this agreement has been exhausted in the above manner, COFA INC agrees to refund the unused portion of the above stated amount. This refund will be issued, when, in the opinion of the then presiding Trustees, funds are available. It may be refunded in one lump-sum, or in monthly payments. By virtue of this agreement, no interest shall be earned or paid. In the event the member does not resign, but becomes an ÒInactive MemberÓ as defined in the COFA Bylaws, a credit shall be maintained as with any other ÒInactive Member."
